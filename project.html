Our Ongoing Projects

Street Kids Feeding Program

We provide daily nutritious meals to street children, helping them regain health and hope for the future.

Skill Training Workshops

Offering skills like carpentry, tailoring, and computer literacy to empower street youth for self-reliance.

Temporary Shelter Program

We offer safe temporary shelters for children rescued from the streets, giving them a chance to rebuild.

Education Support Initiative

We sponsor tuition, uniforms, and learning materials to get street children back to school.

Restore Hope for street children

Bringing hope and a future to street children through care, education, and empowerment.
